# AstroBuild

### AI-Powered Construction Intelligence Platform

> **Transforming Construction Procurement with Artificial Intelligence**

---

# Overview

AstroBuild is an AI-powered Construction Intelligence Platform designed to solve one of the construction industry's most persistent challenges: inefficient procurement and reactive project management.

Construction projects generate enormous amounts of procurement, inventory, supplier, and project data. However, decisions are still largely manual, leading to delayed procurement, material shortages, cost overruns, poor supplier selection, and reduced project visibility.

AstroBuild transforms this process by introducing Artificial Intelligence into every stage of construction procurement. Rather than acting as another project management tool, AstroBuild functions as an intelligent decision-support platform that predicts risks, recommends optimal procurement strategies, and enables stakeholders to make faster, data-driven decisions.

---

# The Problem

Construction projects commonly experience:

* Budget overruns caused by inefficient procurement.
* Material shortages delaying project execution.
* Poor supplier selection due to lack of performance insights.
* Manual procurement workflows that consume significant time.
* Limited visibility into inventory and warehouse status.
* Reactive management instead of predictive planning.

According to industry reports, procurement-related inefficiencies contribute significantly to project delays and increased construction costs. Existing software platforms primarily record project data but rarely provide intelligent recommendations for improving decision-making.

---

# Our Solution

AstroBuild combines Artificial Intelligence, predictive analytics, and centralized project management into a single platform.

Instead of simply displaying project information, AstroBuild continuously analyzes procurement, inventory, supplier, and budget data to generate intelligent recommendations that help organizations reduce costs, improve efficiency, and minimize project risks.

The platform enables construction managers to move from reactive management to predictive decision-making.

## Supplier Intelligence

AstroBuild evaluates suppliers using multiple performance indicators including cost, delivery reliability, quality, and historical performance to assist procurement teams in selecting the most suitable vendors.

---

## Budget Optimization

The platform analyzes procurement costs and project budgets to recommend optimized spending strategies while maintaining project quality.

---

## Predictive Analytics

Using machine learning models, AstroBuild predicts:

* Material demand
* Supplier delays
* Budget overruns
* Procurement risks
* Project delays

These predictions allow project managers to take preventive actions before issues occur.

---

## AI Document Intelligence

Construction projects involve numerous documents including Bills of Quantities (BOQs), purchase orders, invoices, contracts, and delivery notes.

AstroBuild automatically extracts information from these documents, summarizes content, identifies missing fields, and provides procurement insights using OCR and Natural Language Processing.

---

## Executive Dashboard

The executive dashboard provides a real-time overview of:

* Active projects
* Procurement status
* Budget utilization
* Inventory levels
* Supplier performance
* Risk indicators
* AI-generated recommendations

This enables management teams to monitor the health of multiple projects from a single interface.

---

# How AstroBuild Works

1. Users create and configure construction projects.
2. Procurement and inventory data are continuously collected.
3. AI models analyze project information in real time.
4. Predictive analytics identify risks and future resource requirements.
5. The platform generates recommendations for procurement, budgeting, supplier selection, and inventory management.
6. Project managers use these insights to make informed decisions and reduce operational risks.
